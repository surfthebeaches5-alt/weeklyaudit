III
80 / 20 Review
What worked vs. what didn't

This is a pattern analysis, not a list of what happened. Look at your habits and behaviors from the week and ask: what was actually moving the needle, and what was just noise, friction, or avoidance? The 80% side is where most of your energy probably went — but didn't serve you. The 20% side is the small handful of things that genuinely moved you forward. Identify them so you can repeat them. This is separate from your wins and downfalls — it's less about outcomes and more about what to cut and what to double down on.

✗ 80% — What Didn't Help

✓ 20% — What Worked
